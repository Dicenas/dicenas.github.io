The Snow Ain’t Fallin’, But It Stay Real for the Hustlers
Snow ain't hit the ground, but my wrist on froze.
Heart iced out, cold world, you know how it goes.
Holidays hit, but the hustle don’t pause,
Bricks for Christmas - call me Santa Claus.

No sled, no reindeer, but I’m still on route.
Homie on the wheel, pole ready to shoot.
No love, the way my heat stay tucked inside.
Slide a pack down the chimney, watch that product glide.

Like Ho! Ho! Ho!
I make it Snow! Snow! Snow!
You feel me?
Jingle bells - nah that's just the sound of my phone.

Make that snow fall, make that white flake blow.
Watch me ball hard, make them bands flow.

Snowman dreams,
Got the sticks on me.
Got the switch on me.
Got them bricks on me.

Lighting up a square,
Told 'em get that block glowing.
Snuff a Camel in the snow,
Red dots still glowing.

Keep calm and mew on.
